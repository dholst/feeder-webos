Help
FeedSpider is a fork of Semicolon Apps' excellent Google Reader app Feeder, adapted to work with Google Reader compatible applications.

It currently supports:
  • BazQux Reader
  • Feedly
  • InoReader
  • Tiny Tiny RSS
  • The Old Reader
Reach us through:
  • twitter
  • email
  • web
